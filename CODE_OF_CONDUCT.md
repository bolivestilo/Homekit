# Code of Conduct

    ## Our Pledge

    We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the Homekit community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

    ## Our Standards

    **Positive behavior includes:**
    - Using welcoming and inclusive language
    - Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
    - Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
    - Focusing on what is best for the community
    - Showing empathy towards other community members

    **Unacceptable behavior includes:**
    - The use of sexualized language or imagery
    - Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
    - Public or private harassment
    - Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

    ## Enforcement

    Instances of abusive behavior may be reported via [GitHub's private security advisory](https://github.com/bolivestilo/Homekit/security/advisories/new). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
